hi, i'm anyun chatterjee (pronounced "ah-noon"). i'm a phd student and researcher working at the intersection of public health, rhetoric, and decision-making.


i study how people navigate health information and how public health institutions can be influential on the behaviors of the groups they serve. i work at temple university and children’s hospital of philadelphia, using mixed methods to study rhetoric and information processing.

right now, i'm dissertating on what cues create people's gut reactions to health messages. i'm also leading grant-funded research on how public health institutions shape their messaging.
