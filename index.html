hi, i'm anyun chatterjee (pronounced "ah-noon"). i'm a phd student and researcher trying to understand and improve how health communications and health services are delivered.


i study how people navigate health information and how public health institutions can be influential on the behaviors of the groups they serve. i work at temple university and children’s hospital of philadelphia, using mixed methods to study rhetoric, information processing, and public health program implementation.

right now, i'm preparing to defend a dissertation proposal on the evaluations individuals make of institutional messengers in a health communication context. additionally, i am wrapping up a rhetoric analysis of the psa archive i built over summer 2025.

i'm also a passionate educator, with a student-centered philosophy which i expand upon in my blog and teaching page.

you can check out my curriculum vitae (cv) for an overview of past work.
